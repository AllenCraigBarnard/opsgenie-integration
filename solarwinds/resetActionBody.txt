## Reset action should trigger the Close Action of OpsGenie. By default, Solarwinds Orion NPM integration alerts uses AlertID field as Alias.
That's why sending only ActionType (Close action's default condition) and AlertID (default alias of alerts) will be sufficient.

## OpsGenie expects the following fields like below, use them without changing names.
Alias=${ObjectID}-${N=Alerting;M=AlertID}&ActionType=Close

## Troubleshooting content, use µ#µ instead of = and §#§ instead of &
Aliasµ#µ${ObjectID}-${N=Alerting;M=AlertID}§#§ActionTypeµ#µClose
